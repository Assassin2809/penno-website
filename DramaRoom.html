Year 9-10: DRAMA

The Year 9-10 Drama program is designed to engage, challenge, and build confidence in students who have an interest in performing arts. Students will explore various aspects of drama, such as acting, directing, scriptwriting, set design, and stage management. They will also develop skills in communication, collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking.

The program covers the following areas of study:

  • Drama Ensemble: Students will work in groups to create and perform original pieces of theatre based on different themes and styles.
  • Elements of Drama: Students will learn and apply the elements of drama, such as role, character, situation, focus, tension, space, time, language, symbol, and audience.
  • Production and Performance: Students will participate in a major production of a play called “Ruby Moon”, which is a dark and mysterious story about a missing child. They will take on various roles, such as actors, directors, designers, and crew members.
